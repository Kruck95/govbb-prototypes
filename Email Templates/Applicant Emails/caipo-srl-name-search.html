Application received

Dear [Applicant name],

Thank you for submitting your Society with Restricted Liability Name Search (Form 15) through the Government of Barbados online portal. We have received your application.

SRL-[XXXXXX]

Your reference number — keep this safe. You may need it if you contact us about your application.

What happens next

  • A CAIPO officer will search the register for your proposed SRL name.
  • You will be told within 3 working days whether your proposed name is available.
  • If approved, the name will be reserved for a limited period.
  • You must complete your SRL registration within the reservation period.
  • Keep your reference number safe in case you need to follow up.

If you have any questions, contact CAIPO at caipo@caipo.gov.bb or call 246-228-7063.

This is an automated confirmation from the Government of Barbados.

Please do not reply to this email.
