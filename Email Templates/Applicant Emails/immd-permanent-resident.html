Permanent Resident Registration — Applicant Confirmation

Sent to the applicant after they submit the Permanent Resident Registration form.

Application received

Dear [First Name],

Thank you for submitting your Permanent Resident Registration. We have received your application and will review it shortly.

Reference Number: PRR-20260413-XXXXXX

Please keep this reference number for your records.

What happens next

  • We have received your Permanent Resident Registration application (Form A1).
  • An immigration officer will review your application.
  • Processing usually takes 8–12 weeks.
  • You may be required to attend an interview.
  • You will receive a decision letter at the address you provided.
  • Keep your reference number safe for follow-up enquiries.

This is an automated confirmation email from the Government of Barbados.

If you have any questions, please contact the Immigration Department and include your reference number: PRR-20260413-XXXXXX
